# WinMode — by FirstGrid

**WinMode** is an AI-powered sales enablement assistant for account executives.

It helps reps:
- Prepare for calls with better context.
- Spot qualification and discovery gaps.
- Ask better questions by persona.
- Keep deals aligned with their chosen methodology (MEDDPICC, BANT, etc.).

This repo documents the product direction and workflows for WinMode.

---

## Goals

- Reduce “winging it” on important calls.
- Make it easy to see gaps against a chosen framework.
- Help reps leave every interaction with clear, concrete next steps.
- Support sales managers with more consistent qualification and deal hygiene.
